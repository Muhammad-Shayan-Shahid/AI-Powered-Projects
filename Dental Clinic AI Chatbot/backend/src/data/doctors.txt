BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL CLINIC — OUR DOCTORS

Dr. Sarah Mitchell — General Dentist
Dr. Mitchell has 12 years of experience in general and family dentistry. She earned her DDS from the University of Michigan and completed a residency in general dentistry. She specializes in preventive care, fillings, and routine cleanings, and sees patients of all ages including young children. Dr. Mitchell is known for a calm, gentle approach with anxious patients.

Dr. James Okafor — Orthodontist
Dr. Okafor has 9 years of experience in orthodontics, focusing on braces and clear aligner treatment for teens and adults. He completed his orthodontic specialty training at NYU College of Dentistry. He leads all braces and Invisalign cases at the clinic and offers free initial orthodontic consultations.

Dr. Priya Nair — Endodontist
Dr. Nair has 15 years of experience specializing in root canal treatment and other procedures involving the tooth's interior. She trained at the University of Pennsylvania School of Dental Medicine. She handles all root canal cases and complex tooth pain diagnoses referred from the general dentistry team.
