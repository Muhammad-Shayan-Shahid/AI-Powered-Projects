BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL CLINIC — INSURANCE AND POLICIES

Accepted insurance
The clinic accepts Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and Guardian. Patients with other providers can still be seen; the clinic will provide a receipt for out-of-network reimbursement claims. Insurance coverage is verified before treatment when possible, but patients are encouraged to confirm their own benefits with their provider beforehand.

Payment plans
For treatments over $500, the clinic offers a 3-month or 6-month interest-free payment plan. Patients can also use CareCredit financing for larger procedures like implants or orthodontics. Payment is due at the time of service unless a payment plan has been arranged in advance.

Cancellation policy
Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance at no charge. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment, or no-shows, may incur a $50 fee.

New patient policy
New patients should arrive 15 minutes early to complete intake paperwork, or can fill it out online in advance. A first visit typically includes a full exam, X-rays if needed, and a cleaning. New patients are asked to bring a photo ID and insurance card if applicable.

Pediatric care
The clinic welcomes children starting from age 2. Pediatric visits focus on preventive care, cavity checks, and building comfort with dental visits. Parents are welcome to stay in the room during a child's appointment.
