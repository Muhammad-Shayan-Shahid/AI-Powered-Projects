BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL CLINIC — APPOINTMENT FAQS

What are your hours?
The clinic is open Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm, and Saturday 10am to 2pm. Closed on Sundays and major holidays.

Do you accept walk-ins?
Walk-ins are accepted for emergencies such as severe pain or a broken tooth, subject to availability. For routine care, booking an appointment in advance is recommended to avoid wait times.

What should I bring to my first appointment?
Bring a photo ID, your insurance card if you have one, and a list of any current medications. If you have prior dental X-rays from another provider, bringing those (or having them sent ahead) can help avoid retaking them.

How do I reschedule an appointment?
Appointments can be rescheduled by calling the clinic or through the online booking form, up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any fee.

Do you see children?
Yes, the clinic sees children starting from age 2, with a focus on preventive care and building comfort with dental visits.

Will I need X-rays?
X-rays are typically taken during a new patient's first visit and then periodically, usually once a year, to monitor changes that aren't visible during a regular exam. Patients can decline X-rays, though this may limit what the dentist can diagnose.

How long does a typical appointment take?
Routine cleanings take 30-45 minutes. More involved procedures like root canals or veneer fittings can take 60-90 minutes or longer, and may require multiple visits.
