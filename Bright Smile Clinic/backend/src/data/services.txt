BRIGHT SMILE DENTAL CLINIC — SERVICES

Teeth cleaning
A routine professional cleaning that removes plaque and tartar buildup, polishes teeth, and checks for early signs of decay or gum disease. Recommended every 6 months. Price: $80-120. Duration: 30-45 minutes.

Teeth whitening
Professional in-office whitening using a bleaching gel activated by LED light. Removes stains from coffee, tea, wine, and smoking. Results are visible immediately after the session. Price: $250-400 per session. Duration: 60-90 minutes. Effects typically last 6-12 months.

Root canal treatment
A procedure to remove infected or damaged pulp from inside a tooth, clean the canal, and seal it to prevent further infection. Usually needed when a cavity has reached the tooth's nerve. Price: $600-900 depending on the tooth. Duration: 2 sessions of about 60-90 minutes each.

Dental veneers
Thin, custom-made shells bonded to the front of teeth to improve shape, size, or color. Commonly used for chipped, discolored, or unevenly spaced teeth. Price: $800-1,500 per tooth. Duration: 2 visits, several weeks apart for fitting and placement.

Braces and orthodontics
Traditional metal braces or clear aligners to gradually straighten teeth and correct bite issues. Treatment length varies based on case complexity. Price: $3,000-6,000 for a full treatment course. Duration: 12-24 months of treatment, with monthly check-ins.

Dental implants
A titanium post surgically placed in the jaw to replace a missing tooth root, topped with a custom crown. A long-term solution for missing teeth. Price: $1,500-3,000 per implant. Duration: Multiple visits over 3-6 months to allow healing between stages.

Emergency dental care
Same-day appointments for urgent issues such as severe toothache, broken or knocked-out teeth, or lost fillings. Call the clinic directly for emergency slots — walk-ins are also accommodated when possible. Price varies by treatment needed.
